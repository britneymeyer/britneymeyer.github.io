    • Under my tutelage, your child will be given numerous reading opportunities that are based entirely on their specific needs and abilities including (but not limited to):

    2. Comprehension
    3. Fluency
    4. Accuracy
    5. Vocabulary

    I consider literacy to be the most important skill that a child will obtain throughout their educational career. It is used across all curricula, and it is essential to becoming successful in life. However, literacy is one of the hardest skills to foster in a child. If a child struggles with reading or they acquire a dislike of reading, it can be difficult to encourage them to continue developing their literacy skills.
