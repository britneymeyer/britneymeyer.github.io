About

Hi, my name is Britney, and I am a certified teacher looking to help students be successful in all aspects of their education by providing one-on-one instruction tailored to their developmental needs.

My goal is to foster a love of learning in all students and help their education grow using a variety of teaching strategies and activities that align with their particular learning styles, intelligences, and exceptionalities.

I graduated from the University of Central Missouri three years ago with my Bachelor’s in Elementary Education with a minor in English. Throughout my courses, I acquired over eight years of teaching experience in many different facets of education. I am certified to teach first grade through sixth grade in the state of Missouri. I was also a member of the Honors College, and with the collaboration of a faculty mentor, I created a supplementary educational resource called Content Area Tunes (CATs). CATs are songs written to familiar tunes that help teach students about a particular topic (i.e. Mesopotamian culture). Eventually I want to return to college and get my Master’s Degree in Children’s Literature.

I am constantly reading teaching books and journals to learn better ways to educate students of all types of intelligences and learning styles. I am currently reading “The CAFE Book” written by Gail Boushey and Joan Moser about effective ways to engage students in literacy.
